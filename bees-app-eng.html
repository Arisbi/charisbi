Proyect

BEES APP

BEES is a B2B ecommerce for small to medium-sized retailers. Bees provides an easy way to purchase wholesale products from beers to other products in a simple way and at any time of the day.

Role

Ui screen designs, user flows, arquitecture information, web & mobile documentation, and QA supervision.

Year

January 2023 - September 2023

Tools

Figma, Invision

The challenge

My contributions to this project were to look for any improvements on the flows previously worked by the design team. My specific role was to find and document screens that were in production and improve the user experience according to the design system that was being updated at that time with several product designers. We had a migration from Sketch to Figma and with my team we made improvements to several ui elements. We worked alongside the research team, UX writing, and development teams to improve the information within the app and translate it to other countries.

User research

Thanks to the work made by the research team, we were able to have a better understanding of our user, including the fact that we had at our disposal user pain points, user personas, empathy maps, benchmarks and usability studies.
